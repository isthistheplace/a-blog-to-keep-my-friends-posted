Where 116th Subway Riders Go on Fridays & Weekends

Total estimated average ridership for trips originating at 116 St-Columbia University (1 train) on Fridays, Saturdays, and Sundays. Circle size and color reflect total ridership to each destination across these three days.

About this data: Dataset: MTA Subway Origin-Destination Ridership (data.ny.gov, resource 28vm-gjqr). Values are estimated average ridership per hour, derived by the MTA from fare payment (OMNY/MetroCard) records combined with a statistical model that infers likely destination stations, since the subway system does not record exit taps. Figures are model-based estimates, not exact counts. The map sums these hourly estimates across Friday, Saturday, and Sunday for each destination, across all hours in the date range pulled.
