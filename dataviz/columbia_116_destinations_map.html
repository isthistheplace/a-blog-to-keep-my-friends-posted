Friday vs. Thursday: Where 116th Riders Go

Difference in estimated average ridership (Friday minus Thursday) for trips originating at 116 St-Columbia University (1 train).

About this data: Dataset: MTA Subway Origin-Destination Ridership (data.ny.gov, resource 28vm-gjqr). Values are estimated average ridership per hour, derived by the MTA from fare payment (OMNY/MetroCard) records combined with a statistical model that infers likely destination stations, since the subway system does not record exit taps. Figures are model-based estimates, not exact counts. The map shows the difference between average Friday and average Thursday ridership for each destination, across all hours in the date range pulled.
