A weekend in New York

Update (lore, if you will): Went to the City this weekend to visit Lucia. Went to Williamsburg, did the whole Brooklyn thing (museum, food, cool club, finished off the night in a park near the Manhattan Bridge), did Red Hook the day after having gotten little sleep (iKea ferry, Swedish meat/plant balls, park, pinball museum). Overall my reaction was: how pleasant! Lucia is very sweet and thoughtful, though not sure what the outlook is for the future. It’s not something I’m worrying about though, I think I’ve learned to take the future as it comes. I haven’t shown her the scope of my personality yet but her reaction to the glimpses that she has gotten are encouraging (e.g. when I told her that I would go Conrad! Conrad! Conrad! She got excited and thought that was cute).

I also had dinner with Nicole and Joshua, and went to a Mets game with my dad. Juan Soto hit a grand slam.
